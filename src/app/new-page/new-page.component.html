About Our Company

Sparrow Logistics LTD.

Briefly

Welcome to Sparrow Logistics , your premier partner in the realm of shipping and logistics. Nestled in the vibrant city of Vienna, Austria, our headquarters serve as the hub of our operations, strategically located to cater to a diverse array of clients both locally and internationally. Since our inception in 2016, Sparrow Logistics has remained steadfast in our commitment to excellence, a dedication that has propelled us to serve over +200 businesses and successfully deliver more than +15,000 orders.

Our Capacities

We employ over 2000 specialists in our branch offices United States, France, Spain, United Kingdom, Italy, Canada and many other countries. We provide our customers with a wide range of services including Air Freight, Import/Export Transportation, Overnight Delivery, Customs Clearance Services. Our truck park consists of more than 100 items ranging from 8 to 120 tons.
